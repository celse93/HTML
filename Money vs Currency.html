The 3 functions of money:

  • Medium of exchange: allows specialization in better products and services.
  • Store of value: incentivizes individuals to direct resources to investment instead of consumption.
  • Unit of account: allows economic calculation of profits and losses.

Definition of money according to the Austrian Economic School:

Austrain economists try not to be dogmatic or objectivist in their definition of money. They define it not as a specific good or commodity, but rather whichever money emerges on the market which is freely chosen by the people that transact with it. It's not imposed by any authority/government and its value is defined through market interactions.
